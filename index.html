Our Story

At The Italian Spoon, we believe that food is not just about what’s on the plate, but about the moments shared around it. Since 1997, our family has been welcoming guests with open arms, treating each person who walks through our doors like part of our own. Every dish we serve is crafted with love, inspired by the rich traditions of Italy, and made with the freshest ingredients. From our homemade pasta to the perfect, wood-fired pizza, we pour our hearts into every bite, because we know that a meal is so much more than just food it’s about creating memories. Whether you're here to celebrate life's big moments or simply enjoy a quiet meal, we’re honored to be part of your journey. At The Italian Spoon, you're not just a guest YOU'RE FAMILY.

Menu

The Margherita pizza, with fresh tomato, buffalo mozzarella, and basil, is an explosion of authentic, fresh flavors that celebrates Italian tradition.

Our refreshing lemonade is a perfect blend of tangy lemons and a touch of sweetness, served ice-cold for a revitalizing, crisp taste of summer.

Our pasta is crafted with the finest ingredients, perfectly cooked to al dente perfection, and served with rich, flavorful sauces that bring the taste of Italy to every bite.

Toasted crusty bread topped with a vibrant mix of fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and a drizzle of olive oil – a simple, savory Italian classic Bruschetta.

Crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside, our mozzarella sticks are filled with melted mozzarella cheese and served with a tangy marinara dip for the perfect bite-sized treat.
