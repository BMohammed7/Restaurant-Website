Our Story

A plan made by 4 Business Partners The Italian Spoon was the manifestation of our passion for creating amazing Italian Cusine for everyone to enjoy. We believe in bringing the authentic flavors of Italy right to your table, as Our journey started with a simple idea: Develop and Environment where people can enjoy the greatest Italian cuisine made with love and passion. With only 1 location currentely in Oshawa Ontario, we plan to venture out and bring our foods to other areas with new locations coming soon.
